BHealth Buddy: Hospital Appointment System

  • Client BHealth Buddy
  • Category Applications
  • Date February, 2024

I present the database architecture developed for "BHealth Buddy," a pioneering healthcare chatbot system aimed at revolutionizing appointment scheduling and management. Throughout my role as a Database Developer and Tester, I orchestrated the construction of a robust MongoDB database, meticulously structuring schemas to efficiently store patient data, appointment details, and provider information. Working in tandem with the development team, I engineered seamless API integrations to synchronize the chatbot with hospital systems, ensuring real-time data exchange and smooth functionality. Security was paramount in this healthcare context, and I implemented stringent measures including encryption and access controls to safeguard sensitive patient records.

Rigorous system testing was integral to my role, where I meticulously identified and documented defects, validated fixes, and ensured the overall reliability and performance of the system. This capstone project showcases my adeptness in database design, API integration, and meticulous testing methodologies, underscoring my commitment to excellence in healthcare technology innovation. Through collaborative efforts and a meticulous approach to development and testing, BHealth Buddy emerges as a testament to our team's dedication to creating transformative solutions in the healthcare sector, setting new standards for efficiency, security, and patient care.
