Student Life Insights: Exploring Trends and Patterns in University Experiences

  • Client Data Visualization
  • Category Data Science
  • Date March, 2023

In this university project on student life analysis, our team delved into the complexities of understanding and enhancing the student experience using a rich dataset obtained from Kaggle. Serving as the project lead, I steered our collective efforts towards exploring key facets of student life, ranging from the correlation between extracurricular interests and overall satisfaction to gender-based disparities in stress levels.

Our approach encompassed:

- Thorough data cleaning and preprocessing to ensure the integrity and reliability of our analyses.
- Employing a diverse array of data visualization techniques, including stacked bar charts, tree maps, and hierarchical circle charts, to elucidate complex relationships and trends within the dataset.
- Systematically addressing a series of pertinent research questions, such as the influence of primary program of study on activity participation and the impact of housing type on social interaction efforts.
- Drawing upon statistical analysis and visualization to uncover patterns and correlations, such as the nuanced interplay between academic stress and student satisfaction.

This project not only underscored our team's proficiency in data exploration and visualization but also underscored the importance of leveraging data-driven insights to inform decision-making in higher education settings.
